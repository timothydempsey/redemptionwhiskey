How to Enjoy

Redemption Rye is created in small batches and perfectly aged to enjoy neat but the classic rye taste makes any cocktail a work of art. The flavor is crisp, clean and complex. Big, spicy notes shine through in a Manhattan, Old Fashioned or Sazerac without overpowering the drink.

Whiskey Sour

• 2 part Redemption Rye
• 1 part Sweet Vermouth
• 1-2 dashes Aromatic Bitters
• 1 Cherry
Stir and strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a Luxardo cherry.
